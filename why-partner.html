The Process

How working with us
actually looks

From introduction to shipment, here is a straightforward look at how a new supplier relationship works at JBK Coffee.

1

Introduction & First Contact

Reach out to us via the contact form, email, or WhatsApp. Tell us about your farm, your region, your varieties, and what you have available. There is no formal application — just a conversation.

2

Sample Request

If your coffee matches what we are looking for, we will request a sample — typically 300g to 500g of green coffee. We cover or reimburse reasonable shipping costs for sample submission.

3

Cupping & Evaluation

We cup the sample using SCA protocols. We share the results with you — the score, the notes, and our honest feedback. Even if the lot is not right for us this season, the feedback is yours to keep.

4

Price & Terms Agreement

If we are buying, we agree on price, volume, packaging, and payment terms — all in writing, all clear. No verbal agreements that disappear later. Everything is confirmed before any coffee moves.

5

Purchase & Payment

Coffee ships on agreed terms. Payment is made as contracted — no delays, no excuses. We handle logistics coordination and keep you informed at every stage of the export process.
