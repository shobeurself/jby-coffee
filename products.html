Where We Source

Our four key
growing regions

We currently source exclusively from Colombia, focusing on four departments known for producing some of the country's finest specialty-grade green coffee.

01

Huila

Colombia's most celebrated department. Volcanic soils and a double harvest season yield beans with pronounced sweetness, bright malic acidity, and layered fruit complexity.

1,500 – 2,100 m
Stone Fruit Dark Chocolate Panela
02

Nariño

Extreme altitude near the Ecuadorian border produces some of Colombia's most structured coffees. Long maturation cycles create exceptional density and vibrant citric brightness.

1,800 – 2,300 m
Citrus Red Apple Floral
03

Antioquia

The historic heartland of Colombian coffee. Well-balanced, consistent, and approachable — ideal for blending or as a benchmark single origin with broad commercial appeal.

1,400 – 1,900 m
Caramel Hazelnut Milk Chocolate
04

Cauca

High-altitude growing conditions produce coffees with exceptional clarity and complex profiles. An increasingly sought-after origin among specialty buyers worldwide.

1,700 – 2,100 m
Red Fruit Sweet Clean
Varieties We Accept

Coffee varieties

We work with all major Colombian Arabica varieties. Each brings different cup characteristics — all are welcome.

  • Castillo — Colombia's most widely planted variety. Rust-resistant, balanced, and consistent. Chocolatey and clean.
  • Caturra — Classic specialty variety. Bright acidity, citrus-forward, beloved by specialty roasters worldwide.
  • Tabi — Large-beaned and elegant. Complex, floral, and fruity. Highly prized in premium specialty markets.
  • Bourbon — One of the most respected Arabicas in the world. Sweet, silky, and complex. Commands premium pricing.
  • Pajarito — Rare high-altitude mutation of Caturra. A distinctive selling point for specialty and microlot buyers.
  • Other Arabica varieties — Please inquire. We evaluate each variety on its merits.
Processing Methods

Processing methods

We accept multiple processing methods. Processing significantly affects cup profile — please indicate your method when you contact us.

  • Washed / Fully Washed — Our primary focus. Clean, transparent, terroir-driven. The dominant method in Colombia.
  • Natural / Dry Process — Rarer in Colombia and highly valued. Intensely fruity, heavy body, wine-like complexity.
  • Honey Process — Yellow, Red, or Black honey. Sweetness and body between washed and natural.
  • Anaerobic — Experimental and growing in demand. Inquire with full processing details.
Lot Types

What kind of lots
we purchase

⭐

Microlots

Small, precisely selected batches scoring 87+ SCA. Limited volume, maximum traceability. Minimum 30 kg. Premium pricing applies.

🏡

Single Farm Lots

Coffee from one specific farm with full traceability. Strong demand from specialty buyers who want to know exactly where their coffee grew.

🌄

Regional / Community Lots

Blended from multiple smallholders within the same region. Maintains regional identity and flavor consistency. Common and commercially strong.
