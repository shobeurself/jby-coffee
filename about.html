Our Story

Who We Are

A US-registered green coffee buyer dedicated to sourcing Colombia's finest and delivering it to the world's most discerning roasters.

Our Company

Built to bridge
origin and opportunity

JBY Coffee is a specialty green coffee export company headquartered in Yorba Linda, California, USA. We are a subsidiary of JBY Enterprise, Inc. — a US-registered trading company with operations spanning multiple industries and international markets.

Our focus is simple: source the finest Colombian green coffee and deliver it to premium buyers across Asia and global specialty markets. We believe the best coffee in the world grows in Colombia, and we have made it our mission to share it with the world — while ensuring the producers behind it are treated with the respect and fairness they deserve.

We are not a broker or a middleman. We are a direct buyer — which means when you work with JBY Coffee, you work with us directly, from the first conversation to the final payment.

What We Believe

Our values

Everything we do is guided by four core principles. These are not marketing words — they are the commitments we make to every producer we work with.

  • Transparency
    Clear pricing, honest communication, and no surprises. You always know exactly where you stand with us.
  • Reliability
    Consistent purchase commitments you can plan your business around. When we say we will buy, we buy.
  • Respect
    For the farmers, the land, and the extraordinary craft behind every bag of coffee we purchase.
  • Quality
    We seek the best because our buyers demand the best. Quality is not optional — it is our foundation.
Our Market

Where your coffee goes

JBY Coffee exports to premium roasters and specialty importers across Asia — one of the fastest-growing specialty coffee markets in the world. Our buyers in markets like China, Japan, South Korea, Taiwan, and Southeast Asia are seeking exactly what Colombia produces: traceable, high-altitude, single-origin Arabica of exceptional quality.

When you partner with JBY Coffee, your coffee reaches buyers who genuinely appreciate what makes Colombian coffee extraordinary — and who pay accordingly.
